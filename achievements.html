Our Achievements

Fish Crushing Machine Demonstration to Laoag Kaunlaran Association

The BSME Department awarded and demonstrated the Fish Crushing Machine to the Laoag Kaunlaran Association - Cooperative through the Regional Innovation Inclusive Cooperation Program between DTI, DOST, PRMSU, and the MSMED Council on the 14th of October, 2022. This initiative is part of the TEAM-UP EXTENSION PROGRAM, in line with the THRIVE agenda of the College with the goal: "A single step from COE, a hundred mouths fed in the community through upscaling and upgrading livelihood activities of small and medium enterprises in the Province of Zambales."

October 14, 2022

Orientation and Gender Sensitivity Training

The PRMSU College of Engineering conducted the 2nd Batch of the Orientation and Gender and Development (GAD) / Gender Sensitivity Training (GST) Seminar for Freshmen at the Science and Engineering Laboratory Building (SELB) Annex on October 20, 2022. The speakers for the seminar were Engr. Rue Lei Balicaco and Engr. Cindy Dullas.
